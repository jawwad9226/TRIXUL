Challenges for Bus Organizations

No Fleet Visibility

Once a bus leaves the depot, it's a black box. No real-time location or status data.

Inefficient Deployment

Buses running empty on low-demand routes while others are overflowing.

Revenue Leak

Unoptimized Scheduling: Losing potential revenue because buses aren't where the passengers are.

Manual Data

Decisions are based on yesterday's paper reports, not today's reality.

Why Existing Apps Are Not Enough

Redbus / Chalo

Great for Inter-City booking or Metro cities. Not built for rural daily stops.

Reservation Heavy

Requires seat booking. We need "Hop-on Hop-off" flexibility.

One-Sided

Helps passengers, but gives ZERO tools to the conductor to manage crowds.

Static Timetables

Shows "Scheduled" time, not "Real" time GPS status.

GAP: The "Daily Rural Commuter" is completely ignored.
